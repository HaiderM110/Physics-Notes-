# Dimension of Coefficient of Viscosity ($\eta$)

Coefficient of viscosity ki dimensions nikaalne ke liye hum **Newton’s Law of Viscosity** ka istemal karte hain.

---

### 1. Basic Formula
Viscous force ($F$) ka formula niche diya gaya hai:

$$F = \eta A \frac{dv}{dx}$$

Yahan:
* **$F$**: Viscous force
* **$\eta$**: Coefficient of viscosity
* **$A$**: Area
* **$\frac{dv}{dx}$**: Velocity gradient

---

### 2. Isolate Coefficient of Viscosity ($\eta$)
Formule ko rearrange karne par:

$$\eta = \frac{F}{A \cdot (\frac{dv}{dx})}$$

---

### 3. Dimensional Analysis
Ab har quantity ki dimensions likhte hain:

| Quantity | Formula | Dimensions |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Force ($F$)** | $Mass \times Accel.$ | $[MLT^{-2}]$ |
| **Area ($A$)** | $Length \times Width$ | $[L^2]$ |
| **Velocity Gradient ($\frac{dv}{dx}$)** | $\frac{Velocity}{Distance}$ | $[T^{-1}]$ |

---

### 4. Final Calculation
In values ko formula mein substitute karne par:

$$[\eta] = \frac{[MLT^{-2}]}{[L^2][T^{-1}]}$$

Isse simplify karne ke baad:
* $M$ ka power: $1$
* $L$ ka power: $1 - 2 = -1$
* $T$ ka power: $-2 - (-1) = -1$

### **Final Result:**
Coefficient of viscosity ($\eta$) ki dimensional formula hai:

> **$$[\eta] = [ML^{-1}T^{-1}]$$**

---
*Note: SI unit of $\eta$ is **Pa·s** or **kg/(m·s)**.*
